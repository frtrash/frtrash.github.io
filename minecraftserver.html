Meteor Community Rules

By joining our server, you agree to follow these rules!

These are the most important rules. For complete rules, visit our Discord server

General Rules

Be respectful. Bullying, insults, or harassment have no place here.

Do not advertise other servers, social media, or suspicious links.

Staff has the final say. For disputes, open a ticket—no arguments.

Do not exploit any gameplay advantages unfairly.

Minecraft Rules

Hacked clients, x-ray, autoclickers, and similar tools are prohibited.

Do not use redstone or farms that cause lag. No bugs, exploits, or duplication.

Do not grief the world, troll, or burden the server with destructive behavior.

PvP is prohibited outside of special arenas or events unless mutually agreed upon.
